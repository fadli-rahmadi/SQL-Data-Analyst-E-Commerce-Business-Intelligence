# 💳 SQL Data Analyst | E-Commerce Business Intelligence

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes transactional data from the Olist Brazilian E-commerce dataset using SQL to uncover customer purchasing behavior, regional sales performance, and payment patterns. The objective is to transform raw relational data into actionable business insights that support customer retention strategies and revenue optimization.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

As one of Brazil's largest e-commerce marketplaces, Olist processes thousands of transactions daily. However, the company needs to answer several key business questions:

- Who are the highest-value customers?
- Which states contribute the highest revenue?
- Which customer segments should be prioritized?
- How do payment behaviors influence business performance?

This project addresses these questions using SQL-based data exploration and business intelligence analysis.

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Source:** Olist Brazilian E-commerce Dataset

### Tables Used

- `olist_orders`
- `olist_customers`
- `olist_order_payments`

The three tables are connected using relational keys to analyze customer transactions, payment information, and geographic distribution.

## 📊 Key Findings

- Champion and Loyal Customers generate the highest revenue.
- One customer contributes significantly more revenue than other customers.
- São Paulo (SP) is the highest revenue-generating state.
- Customer segmentation enables more effective retention strategies.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Launch cashback and voucher programs for high-value customers.
- Provide VIP treatment for top-spending customers.
- Prioritize marketing investment in São Paulo.
- Improve delivery services in high-performing regions.
- Continuously monitor customer segments and regional performance.
